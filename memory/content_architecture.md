## 2. Multi-Channel Distribution (The Blitz)
Social media is not just for traffic; it is for **Asset Repurposing**. 

*   **The Text Core:** X, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon, Substack Notes. (Forensic verdicts and "Telling Physics Stories" snippets).
*   **The Visual Hook:** Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest. (Image-driven worldbuilding aesthetics and diagrams).
*   **The Audio/Deep-Dive:** Substack/Spotify (NotebookLM Podcasts), YouTube (Static Video + Effects).
